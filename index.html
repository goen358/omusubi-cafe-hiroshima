What is Omusubi Cafe Hiroshima?

Omusubi Cafe Hiroshima is a handmade rice-ball specialty cafe that uses local ingredients from Hiroshima, Miyajima, and Onomichi.
We share the charm of our region through carefully crafted, comforting omusubi.

Features of Omusubi Cafe Hiroshima

We value quiet awareness, gentle connection, and the beauty of everyday life.
Our activities are based on the Being Model philosophy.
